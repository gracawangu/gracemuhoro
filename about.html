Bio

An analyst with interests in research, data analytics, and visual storytelling. She support data-driven decisions and policies through translating research into actionable insights and turning insights into tangible impact.

She holds an MA in Economics from the University of Nairobi and her research work has been featured by a Think Tank (Kenya Institute for Public Policy Research and Analysis), Journal of World Economic Research and in public policy reports that have informed policymaking including a project supported by World Bank’s Kenya Accountable Devolution program (funded by Danida, European Union, Finland, SIDA-Sweden, UK Aid and USAID) on “Public Sector Wage Bill Study: Lessons Learned from State Corporations and County Governments."
